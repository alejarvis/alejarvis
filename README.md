## About me

I'm an Italian full stack developer and founder of **Binancy**, a software house focused on building custom digital solutions for companies, professionals, and small businesses.

I mainly work on:

- professional websites
- custom CRM platforms
- business management software
- Laravel-based web applications
- API integrations and automations
- VoIP, SIP, WebRTC and call systems
- deployment, servers, domains, DNS and infrastructure

I like building practical software that solves real business problems.

---

## What I build

### CRM & business software

I build custom platforms to manage customers, orders, tasks, teams, documents, payments, workflows and internal business processes.

### Laravel web applications

I develop modern web applications using **Laravel**, **Vue**, **Inertia.js**, **MySQL** and clean, responsive interfaces.

### Automation & AI tooling

I create tools to automate repetitive tasks, integrate third-party services and improve business productivity.

### VoIP & call systems

I work with VoIP configurations, FreePBX, WebRTC, SIP providers and call systems integrated into business software.
